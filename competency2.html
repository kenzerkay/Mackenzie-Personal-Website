Core Competency 2: Creating Effective Learning Environments

Description of Competency

Competency 2 focuses on creating inclusive and equitable spaces where students are able to grow and learn effectively. I completed this competency during the Certification in College Teaching Institute in the summer of 2025, which is a professional development seminar series aimed at helping students completing the Certification process.

Artifact from CCT Summer Institute

For my 2nd competency, I have chosen to highlight the presentation "Creating Effective Learning Environments" by Dr. Kristin Parkin. In this resource, Dr. Parkin discusses the importance of creating inclusive and equitable learning environments and provides strategies for achieving this goal. We should aim to create classrooms where students feel safe and supported so that they can push their mental boundaries and make mistakes. The presentation covers topics such as understanding student diversity and fostering a sense of belonging among students.
