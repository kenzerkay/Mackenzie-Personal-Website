Code 4 Community

I was a very active member of the computer science club Code 4 Community at Ohio State Univeristy, serving first as treasurer and then as president of the club, each for one year. The main goal of this student organization was to introduce K-12 students to the fundamentals of computer science. I helped developed multiple modules and led over a dozen outreach presenations at local schools and after school programs in central Ohio. If you are looking for something fun to play, I recommend Cats & Mice which is a program I helped develop to teach studnet about variables.
